Tips
  • Click on the text "Player ratings" to refresh all tables
  • Click on the heading "Lichess" to refresh the Lichess table and sort by player list
  • Click on the heading "Chess.com" to refresh the Chess.com table and sort by player list
  • Click on any other heading to sort by rating
  • Click on the "↑↓" button to change the order of tables
  • if you see the check mark "✔" before a player's name, it means that this player is online
  • Click on User button and then Registration/Lichess to save the player names and other settings to the server. These settings will be available for all your devices!
