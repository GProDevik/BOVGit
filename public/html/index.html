Tips
  • Click on the text "Player ratings/score" to refresh all tables
  • Click on the heading "Lichess" to refresh the Lichess table and sort by player list
  • Click on the heading "Chess.com" to refresh the Chess.com table and sort by player list
  • Click on any other heading to sort by rating
  • Click on the "↑↓" button to change the order of tables
  • For Lichess there are several types of player status to the left of his name in table:
    • ✔ - online
    • ● - playing
    • 🎤 - streaming
  • If you hover the mouse over the player in table, a pop-up window will appear with an information about player
